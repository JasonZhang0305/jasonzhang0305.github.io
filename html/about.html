Study & Research


I studied marketing when I was an undergraduate student, and now I'm studying public economics in graduate school of Hitotsubashi University. In college, I not only learned academic knowledge in lessons, but also studied some practical skills on my own. For example, I learned programming out of class. The first programming language I learned was python. It finally proved to be very useful in my study and resaerch. Since the period of undergraduate student, I had been interested in solving practical problems with data. I used data analysis skills in several internship and got relatively deep insight in some fields, such as consulting, digital platform service and marketing. Besides, I am also triligual in Chinese, English and Japanese, and I like communicating with people having different bankground.


Recently, I'm conducting my research about the relationship between the new technologies and public welfare. I want to find some evidence from data to explore how the introduction of automation devices such as AI and robot affects the staff working in elderly nursing home. Since, nowadays many people worry about that the AI will probably replace their jobs. Actually, it is a very controversial problem that whether robot and AI decrease the employment or not, especially in service sectors. Therefore, I want to acquire strong data and use appropriate method to verify this problem.
